# Subspaces
## SubSpace of $R^n$
### Subsets and Subspaces
A subset of $R^n$ is any collection of vectors that are in $R^n$.  
For example,
- the span of the columns of a $3 \times 4$ matrix is a subset of $R^3$.
- the set of all vectors of the form $(1,k)$ is a subset of $R^2$.

A subset $H$ of $R^n$ is a subspace, if it is closed under scalar multiplies
and vector addition.
For example,
$$
c \vec{u} \in H \\[3pt]
\vec{u} + \vec{v} \in H
$$
Note that condition 1 implies that the zero vector must be in H.
